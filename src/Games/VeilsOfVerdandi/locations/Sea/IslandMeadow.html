Before you lies an island meadow. The meadow is full of beautiful flowers, growing wild. Between the flowers, blossoming fruit trees cast their shade.

The air is permeated with a flowery scent, and stirs with the movement of small insects flying from flower to flower. There is a small path running through the flowers. Evidence that the island might not be deserted.

As you walk down the small path, a drowsiness overcomes you. The sight of the colourful flowers, the glistening sunlight, the lazy movements of the insects and the heady perfume, instils a feeling of relaxation and dreaminess within you.

A feeling which suddenly vanishes, as you spot a strange castle in the distance.
