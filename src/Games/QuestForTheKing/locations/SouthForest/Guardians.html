As you move through the forest, you have the sensation of being watched. But there is no one there. As you travel further, the sensation intensifies.

From the corners of your eyes, you see silhouettes of strange faces appear in the bark of the trees. Gnarled branches extend into grasping arms. But as you turn, there is nothing there. Then, suddenly, the trees in front of you bend over. They grasp at each other with their branches, and intertwine them. The path before you is utterly blocked.

As you speak the strange word mentioned in the parchment, the branches of the trees untwist. The trees bent back, and resume their upright positions. The way before you is clear.
