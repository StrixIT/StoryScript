The waves disappear, and the surface of the ocean becomes smooth, mirroring the cloudless sky. The only thing you hear are the sounds of seagulls, far in the distance.

Suddenly, the surface is disturbed by a small ripple. Another follows, closer to the ship. The ripples begin to deepen, growing larger and forming small whirlpools. The whirlpools grow in size and speed up their rotations.

Suddenly, the ocean surface is broken with thunderous splashes. All around you huge red tentacles shoot out from the deep. The tentacles latch themselves unto the hull and deck of the ship with red suckers. The ship begins to shake and tremble, as if under tremendous stress.

Some distance from the ship, you observe a large, dome shaped head emerge above the surface of the water. It has two, large green eyes which look at you with intelligence. As the creature observes you, some of its red tentacles detach from the ship, and begin to flail menacingly in your direction.
