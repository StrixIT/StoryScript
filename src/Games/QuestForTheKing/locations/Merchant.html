You enter a forest clearing. A colourful wagon stands in the centre of the clearing. In front of the wagon, you can see a circle of stones and within it, the remains of a camp fire. The door to the wagon is closed, there seems to be no one about.

As you are about to move on, you hear a sudden cracking sound, followed by a faint whisper. ´Pssst. Come over here.´ The door to the wagon has opened slightly. As you approach it, the door opens more wide. From the shadows emerges a middle-aged man. The man is wrapped in a dark cloak, hiding all but his leather boots. The man has short black curls, dark skin and a large black moustache.

‘I normally don´t do business at such an ungainly hour. But you look like you have gold to spare.´ The man smiles, bearing a number of gold teeth. ‘And whatever you need, friends, I can provide. Weapons and armour. Swords and bows of the highest quality. And magic items. Strange, mysterious items of such power as to be found nowhere else. Oh, and I can teach you a skill or two, if you are of a certain profession.’ The man grins, and awaits your choice.
