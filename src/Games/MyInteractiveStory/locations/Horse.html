You take a few steps towards the horse, but then trip over what is perhaps a root and fall towards the animal. With effort, you manage to grab a bridle. The horse is disturbed and pulls back it's head, putting you back on your feet with force. There is a flash of pain in your back, but before you really notice it you make out a number of red eyes between the dark trees.

Out of the darkness comes a terrifying howl. It makes your blood run cold, and the horse cries out in fear. As your thoughts scramble through your head, you spot a sword strapped to the saddle. Will you let go of the bridle and try to grab the sword, or get on the horse's back and make a run for it?
