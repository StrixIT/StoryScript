At nightfall on the 9th day, you have arrived at a strange chasm, a jagged fissure in the earth. The fissure seems to lead down into a system of caves. The wizard has been barely able to contain his excitement, and has asked a volunteer to go down into the chasm right away, to do some scouting.

You have volunteered, eager to show your ability and daring. As soon as you descended into the darkness of the fissure however, there was a strange sound. From above, you heard an unearthly voice, followed by the howls of strange beasts. Then, a rallying cry from the ranger Bragi, and the wizard shouting magical incantations. The sounds of a furious battle reached you.

Then, a great silence followed. Suddenly, the rope with which you descended was cut, and you fell down. Down into the darkness.
