You have become a healer and a druid. A witch, according to some, and have spend your days exploring the mysteries of the forest and the magic of the circle of life.

But your days of peace are numbered, as a darkness looms at the horizon. A darkness which has started to plague your dreams of late, with images of death and doom. You have a feeling the source of the darkness is near. The great lake, something has happened, something has awoken. An ancient, and powerful malignant force.

The village must be warned, must be protected. But first, you must find out the nature of the dark force, and investigate the great lake.
