You come upon a jungle pond, with a rustling waterfall. Lilypads float on the surface, and between them grow delicate white flowers. Beneath the surface the shadows of fish dart to and fro.

On the banks surrounding the pond grow jungle reeds and large ferns. There is a quiet about the place, jungle birds sit quietly in the canopy, and the movement of jungle creatures in the underbrush seems subdued as well.

It feels as if the struggle for growth and survival, so present in the jungle, is laid to rest for a moment.
