You are standing in the centre of the ships deck.

Near you is the ships mast, holding the great blue sail with the white eye, that now flutters rather powerlessly in a soft ocean breeze.
At the railing you can see one of the crewmembers of the ship, Vigga.

Vigga seems lost in thought, staring out across the sea. She is dressed in a leather jacket and short leather pants, bare foot as usual. On her head, she wears a red scarf. From beneath it, long strands of blond hair fall across her shoulders.
