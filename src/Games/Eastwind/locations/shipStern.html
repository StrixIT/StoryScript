You are at the stern of the ship.

Behind a great wooden steering wheel, a flamboyant looking sailor with greyish hair and green eyes is holding the ship on course. He wears a worn leather jacked and an equally worn red shawl around his neck.
With the lack of wind, he seems to have little to do. He rests his elbows on the wheel and smokes a crooked pipe.

Some distance before the steering wheel, you can see an iron hatch, allowing entrance into the bowls of the ship.
