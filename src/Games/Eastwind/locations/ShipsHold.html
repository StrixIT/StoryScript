You have entered into the ships hold. It's quite dark around you. But you can make out the shapes of boxes and crates, holding goods of various kinds. There are also some large seamen's chests, locked with iron padlocks.
The atmosphere is quite is clammy and dank, and the murmur of the sea and wind has fallen away. Instead, you hear the sound of creaking wood, and the dripping of water.

From here, you can go to the front and aft of the hold.
