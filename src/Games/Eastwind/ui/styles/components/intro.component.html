Game introduction

'Welcome, friend. My name is Fasold the storyteller, and I thank you for taking the time to meet up with me in this tavern. I have observed your actions for some time, and I am convinced there is greatness within you, and with the adventure before you tomorrow, there shall be much to write down and chronicle for the ages. So please, take a seat and tell me all about you, for a good a historian and storyteller must know the subject of his writings.'
